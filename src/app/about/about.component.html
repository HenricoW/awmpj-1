This Platform

... allows you to take advantage of the combination of a trading bot applied to crypto charts. This process has been refined into a cryptocurrency offering that had its initial coin offering (ICO) successfully complete at the end of last year. The crypto offering is known as BitConnect (BCC) and its main purpose is to allow participants to profit by various means, more so than with other cryptocurrencies (by mining, holding, staking, lending and trading) from the digital currency.

Since their launch in November 2016, the BitConnect coin has surged up to the top 20 cryptocurrencies with a market cap of over half a billion dollars (see Coin Market Cap, see Coin Dance). Astute cryptocurrency followers will remember that in the build-up to the bitcoin cash launch near the start of August 2017, it was referred to as BCC. After the launch the ticker of bitcoin cash was changed to BCH - the official existence of BitConnect was the reason for this.
