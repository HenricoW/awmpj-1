The Cryptocurrency Revolution

Cryptocurrencies are starting to make an impact on global economics with the total global market cap approaching $ 200 billion. Not all are used as currency, most alt coins represent a token of a blockchain-based startup. Many alt coins can be used as a store of value because of growing demand and limited supply.

This website aims to spread cryptocurrency adoption and to kick off your research into emerging opportunities. One such opportunity is Bit Connect.

Bit Connect started from a community of early bitcoin users in Asia that wanted to trade and share ideas. The community grew and lead to the creation of a new coin at the end of 2016 (Bit Connect ICO announcement). Like all alt coins, value is derived from perceived value and widespread adoption for trade. Based on the existing community of users, adoption grew quickly, rising past over 500 new alt coins to the top 15 alt coins in the world (coin market cap) with a market cap of close to $ 1 billion.

How to profit

Methods 1 & 2: Mining/Minting

BitConnect Coin uses special algorithms called the POW/POS to secure the BitConnect Coin network. The moment you acquire BitConnect Coin it becomes an interest bearing asset with, currently, 50% return per 6 months through PoS minting. All you have to do to earn with this method is to hold coins in your BitConnect-QT wallet. In addition to PoS minting, BitConnect Coin can be mined with a CPU/GPU and does not need an ASIC miner like Bitcoin does.

Method 3: Lending

This method is unique to bitconnect and uses a combination of POS minting and a trading bot operating on the BTC/USD and other currencies data. Bitconnect coins are borrowed to the trading bot. It keeps these coins in its wallet it uses to trade. Profits are distributed proportionally to those who borrowed. Losses are limited by Method 2 (POS) from the funds held in the trading bot's wallet.
