Account Setup

Here you will get your account ready and secure. Let's go.

Activate bitconnect account

You now have a brand new BitConnect account.

BitConnect will soon send you an e-mail asking you to activate your account. When you do, come back to this page and click the "I have activated my BitConnect account" button.

Change your BitConnect password

Secure your account with a new password

Shortly we will send you the temporary password used to create your BitConnect account. It is very important that you change this as soon as possible. When you receive the e-mail with your temporary password, log in to BitConnect with it, then go to 'Security' (lock icon) under the menu and follow the instructions under 'CHANGE PASSWORD'. Please use a different password from the one you used on this website. Once you have done so, come back to this page and click the button below.

Set up 2FA

Add an extra layer of security to your BCC account.

Log in to your BitConnect account, go to 'Security' (lock icon) under the menu and follow the instructions under 'TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION'. Once you have set up two-factor authentication with your phone, come back to this page and click the button below.

Success!

Your brand new BitConnect account is now secure. Please remember your password and keep your code card in a safe place. You will need the code card when making withdrawals.

Create a Code Card

Secure your withdrawals.

To be able to withdraw from BitConnect, you will need a Code Card. Log in to your BitConnect account, go to 'Security' (lock icon) under the menu and follow the instructions under 'CODE CARD'. When you do, come back to this page and click the button below.

Address

Add your BCC address for withdrawals and deposits.

In order for funds to reflect in your BCC account, we need to know what your bcc account address is. Log in to your BitConnect account. On the dashboard (top icon on the left), click on 'Deposit BCC'. This will show your BCC address. Copy that address, paste it here and click "Set my BCC address"
